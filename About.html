    • About Us

    Welcome to Dawson's Car Dealership - the ultimate destination for automobile enthusiasts with a taste for the exquisite and the powerful. Our journey into the world of rare and sports cars began with a single wrench, a set of skilled hands, and a passion for transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary.

    The Founder

    Dawson Mercier, our esteemed founder, isn't just a name behind a brand; he's the very heartbeat of this establishment. With grease on his hands and determination in his soul, Dawson embarked on his automotive journey in 2003 as a car mechanic. His exceptional skill and relentless pursuit of perfection quickly set him apart in the world of automotive repair.



    But Dawson's ambitions revolved around not just fixing cars, but reviving them. In 2013, he shifted gears to focus on restoring and selling used rare cars. Dawson's touch brought back the lost glory of classic beauties that once roared on the highways and racetracks, and his small garage became a haven for rare finds.

    The Evolution

    Dawson's Car Dealership is not just a dealership; it's the culmination of years of hard work, an undying love for cars, and a vision that looks beyond the sale. Here, each vehicle tells a story, and each purchase is the beginning of a thrilling new chapter for our clients.

    As our reputation grew, so did our collection. Today, we proudly offer an array of new sports cars that promise not just a mode of transportation, but an experience. With Dawson's keen eye for quality and performance, every car in our showroom is a masterpiece waiting for its next connoisseur.
